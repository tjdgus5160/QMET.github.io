Prof. Kim Minjun

Lab Director & Professor

Prof. Kim Minjun is a leading researcher in deep learning and computational intelligence with over 15 years of experience in academia and industry. He received his Ph.D. from MIT in 2008 and joined Seoul National University in 2015, where he founded the Computational Intelligence Lab.

His research has been published in top-tier venues including NeurIPS, ICML, CVPR, and Nature Machine Intelligence. He has received numerous awards including the NSF CAREER Award, Google Faculty Research Award, and Best Paper Awards at major conferences.

Research Interests
Deep Learning Computer Vision Meta-Learning AI Safety
