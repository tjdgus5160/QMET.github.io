Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions we receive

How do I apply for a PhD position?

Applications for PhD positions are handled through the Seoul National University graduate admissions system. We recommend reaching out to Prof. Kim Minjun directly with your CV and research interests before applying. Application deadlines are typically in December for Fall admission.

Do you accept visiting students or researchers?

Yes, we welcome visiting researchers and students for short-term collaborations (minimum 3 months). Please contact us with your research proposal, CV, and proposed visit dates. Note that visiting positions are typically self-funded or require external funding.

What are the requirements for joining the lab?

We look for candidates with strong backgrounds in computer science, mathematics, or related fields. Proficiency in Python and experience with deep learning frameworks (PyTorch/TensorFlow) is highly valued. Research experience and publications are preferred but not required for master's students.

Can I collaborate with your lab on a research project?

We're always open to research collaborations with academic institutions and industry partners. Please email us with details about your proposed collaboration, including research objectives, timeline, and potential funding sources.

How can I access your published code and datasets?

Most of our code is available on our GitHub organization page. Datasets are typically available through links in our publications. If you need access to specific resources, please contact us directly with details about your research use case.

Do you offer internship opportunities?

We occasionally offer internship positions for exceptional undergraduate students, typically during summer (June-August). These are competitive positions requiring strong programming skills and genuine interest in AI research. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis.
