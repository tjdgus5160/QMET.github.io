Pioneering the future of quantum world

Our lab is dedicated to advancing the frontiers of computational intelligence through rigorous research and innovative applications. We focus on developing novel algorithms and systems that push the boundaries of what's possible in machine learning and AI.

Founded in 2015, we have established ourselves as a leading research group in deep learning, computer vision, and natural language processing. Our work has been published in top-tier venues and has been adopted by industry partners worldwide.

We believe in the power of interdisciplinary collaboration and maintain strong partnerships with leading institutions and companies around the globe.
