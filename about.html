About Our Lab

Advancing the frontiers of artificial intelligence through innovative research, collaborative partnerships, and a commitment to solving real-world challenges.

Who We Are

The Computational Intelligence Lab is a leading research group dedicated to pushing the boundaries of artificial intelligence and machine learning. Founded in 2015 at Seoul National University, our lab has grown into a vibrant community of researchers, engineers, and students working on cutting-edge AI technologies.

Our research spans multiple domains including deep learning, computer vision, natural language processing, reinforcement learning, and robotics. We believe in the power of fundamental research to drive innovation while maintaining a strong focus on practical applications that benefit society.

With a diverse team of 15+ members from various backgrounds, we foster an environment of creativity, collaboration, and continuous learning. Our work has been published in top-tier conferences and journals, and we maintain active partnerships with leading research institutions and companies worldwide.
